VIM SUBSTITUTE PRACTICE
=======================

INSTRUCTIONS:
-------------
1. Read the task for each section.
2. Visually select the indicated lines (use `V` or `v`).
3. Perform the substitution as described.
4. Check your result and undo (`u`) if needed.
5. Move to the next exercise.
6. (Optional) Better display with `:set filetype=help`

---------------------------------
REMINDER: Vim Substitution Basics
---------------------------------
- Substitute in selection: `:s/pattern/replacement/`
- Global (all matches in line): `:s/pattern/replacement/g`
- Capture groups: `\(pattern\)`, use `\1`, `\2`, etc. in replacement
- "Very magic" mode: Start pattern with `\v` to avoid escaping parentheses and other regex characters
  Example: `:s/\v(\w+) (\w+)/\2 \1/`
- Undo: Press `u` in normal mode
- `:noh` to clear old searches
